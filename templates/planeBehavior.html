How to Ride: The Plane
Rules/Behavior
Uniformed Team Members will help you when you need it but they have to take care of the passengers as well! You can be a courteous Traveler by following the safety rules of the plane. Here are some examples:

  1. Keep your seatbelt on when the seatbelt light is on.
  2. Be mindful of your noise level, especially if people are sleeping.
  3. When the flight is over, wait for the Uniformed Team Member to give you directions.
  4. In a time of an emergency, remain calm, breathe, and listen to the directions of the Uniformed Team Member.
By behaving like a helpful Traveler, you will not only have a fantastic flight, but you will make your fellow passengers happy as well!
